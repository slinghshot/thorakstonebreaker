Tharok "Stonebreaker"

Exiled Barbarian

"I was cast out... but I will not let my children grow fatherless, nor my people fade to whispers. I will fight, bleed, and die before I let that happen."

Backstory

Tharok was once the pride of the Ironmaw Clan—a warrior whose strength was matched only by his ambition. His deeds on the battlefield were sung in campfires, his name carried with respect and fear. He took Kaera, a fierce hunter from a neighboring clan, as his wife, and together they raised seven children—each strong, sharp-eyed, and bred for survival. His life seemed carved from legend. Then came the raid that changed everything. Ordered to slaughter a rival clan’s survivors, Tharok hesitated before a cowering family—too reminiscent of his own. His mercy was seen as betrayal. Brought before the clan council, with Kaera watching in silence and his children held back by warriors, he was exiled.


"Mercy weakens us," his father spat. "You are no Ironmaw."


Stripped of his clan marks, Tharok was cast into the wilds. He turned back only once—to see his youngest child reaching out to him, soldiers pulling them away.


His rage boiled beneath his skin, not just for the betrayal but for what it cost him: his family. The Ironmaw claimed to act for the clan’s good—but they robbed his children of their father. Kaera remained with the clan, bound by tradition, though Tharok never forgot the fire in her eyes that night—a mix of sorrow and anger.


Now, Tharok roams the world, seeking glory not for redemption—but for power. If he becomes a legend, the Ironmaw will be forced to remember him. And when he’s strong enough? He’ll return—not begging for acceptance but demanding a reckoning. His children will know his name, whether by stories of his conquests or by his blade cutting through those who kept them apart.

Children:

Goals & Motivations

  • Reunite with his wife and children, regardless of the cost.
  • Restore the Ironmaw Clan to a state of honor and stability.
  • Carve a legendary name that ensures his family’s future safety.
  • Overcome the visions of doom that haunt his nights.
